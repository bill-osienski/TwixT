How to Play:
• Red must cross from top goal line to bottom goal line
• Black must cross from left goal line to right goal line
• Click empty holes to place pegs
• Pegs connect via knight's moves
• Bridges cannot cross
• First to cross their goal lines wins!

Keyboard:
• U - Undo move
• N - New game
• H - Toggle help

How to Play:
• Red must build a connected chain from top to bottom
• Black must build a connected chain from left to right
• Click empty holes to place pegs
• Pegs auto-connect via "knight's moves" (like chess: 2 squares one direction, 1 square perpendicular)
• These connections form bridges between your pegs
• Bridges cannot cross each other
• First to complete a continuous chain across the board wins!

Camera Controls:
• Left Click + Drag - Rotate view
• Right Click + Drag - Pan camera
• Scroll Wheel - Zoom in/out

Keyboard:
• U - Undo move
• N - New game
• H - Toggle help
